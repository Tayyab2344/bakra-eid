عِيدُ الْأَضْحَى مُبَارَك
Eid ul Adha Mubarak
Bakra Eid  ·  2026

May this blessed day of sacrifice fill your heart
with joy, your home with warmth, and your soul
with the peace that comes from true devotion.
May Allah accept your Qurbani & bless you abundantly.

تَقَبَّلَ اللَّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنكُم
Taqabbal Allahu Minna wa Minkum
With warm wishes from
Rana Muhammad Tayyab Atiq
✦   Eid Mubarak to you & your loved ones   ✦
